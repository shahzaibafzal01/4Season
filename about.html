About Us

Poly doors are made from polymers such as polyvinyl chloride (PVC), fiberglass, or other composite materials. We have a lot of designs available in 8 Feet. These materials are known for their strength, resistance to moisture, and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions.They are highly durable and resistant to rotting,warping, rust, and corrosion. This makes them suitable for use in areas with high humidity. Read more

CHARACTERISTICS OF OUR PRODUCTS

  • Excellent dimensional stability
    • In dry or wet weather there will be hardly any shrinkage or expansion of the door panels. The inner and outer
    • joints are not visible; they are made using hydraulic system and special resins.
  • Stable to ultraviolet & infrared rays
    • The stability of door panels and frames was checked by exposing them to direct sunlight, heat and rain for four
    • years. No change in the colour or any failure in the material was observed, proving that they are very stable.
  • Durability
    • These doors are immune to any bacterial growth, fungi, pest and notably to termite attack. They are washable
  • Color Choice
    • A variety of colours are available e.g. White, Off-white, Blue, Yellow, Ivory, Pink or any other shade.
  • Silky Touch
    • The appearance of the product is smooth, silky and everlasting.
  • Doorframes (chogats)
    • The appearance of the product is smooth, silky and everlasting.
    • We also fix doors with steel-frames, which are already installed during the construction of buildings.
    • Our technicians fix doors on site at nominal charges.
