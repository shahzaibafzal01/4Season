DETAILS ABOUT US

  • Material
    • Poly doors are made from polymers such as polyvinyl chloride (PVC), fiberglass, or other composite materials. These materials are known for their strength, resistance to moisture, and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions.
  • Durability
    • They are highly durable and resistant to rotting, warping, rust, and corrosion. This makes them suitable for use in areas with high humidity or extreme weather.
  • Low Maintenance
    • Poly doors typically require minimal maintenance compared to traditional wooden doors. They are easy to clean and often require simple wiping with a mild detergent and water.
  • Versatility
    • These doors come in various styles, designs, and colors, offering homeowners a wide range of options to match their aesthetic preferences. They can mimic the appearance of wood, metal, or other materials.
  • Insulation Properties
    • Some poly doors are designed with insulation properties, providing better energy efficiency by minimizing heat transfer between indoor and outdoor spaces.
  • Security
    • Many poly doors are manufactured with security features, including reinforced cores and lock systems, making them secure options for homes and commercial spaces.
  • Cost
    • While prices may vary based on design and features, poly doors can often be more cost-effective than doors made from certain natural materials like solid wood or metal.
  • Installation
    • They can be relatively easy to install and can come pre-hung in frames, simplifying the installation process for homeowners or contractors.
